Welcome to ProSightful

Are you having significant challenges in your career or relationships, or seeking support in moving through a life or relational challenge? Does it feel like you are stuck in a relationship or a job where you still feel alone and can't move forward? Perhaps, somewhere along the way, you've lost your passion and now you feel like going through the motions. Sometimes we all need help in finding creative solutions or building strong relationships.

ProSightful Counselors are here to help you in taking right steps to achieve your counseling or career goals. We will help you identify areas for change, think outside the box for new insights and solutions and learn tools to manage your life, career and enhance your relationships.

Our goal is to empower you to access your own inner wisdom and resources to move forward. Our holistic approach incorporates body, mind and spirit to help you, your spouse or your family create your full personal and relational potential. We would be honored to support you on your journey.

Our Services

Individual Counseling

We assist individuals, children, teens and adults in exploring their personal, emotional, spiritual, career and psychological issues in a non-judgement caring environment.

Couple Counseling

We assist couples to learn healthy ways to communicate effectively and efficiently in order to create healthy intimacy.

Family Therapy

We provide a solution-focused therapeutic process to assist each family member recognize and value each other.
